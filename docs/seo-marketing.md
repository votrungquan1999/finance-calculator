## Meta Content Templates

### Title Templates:

- **Format**: "[Calculator Name] | Free [Benefit] Tool"
- **Length**: <60 characters
- **Keywords**: Include primary keyword
- **Benefit**: Focus on user benefit

### Title Examples:

- "Investment Calculator | Free Retirement Planning Tool"
- "Debt Payoff Calculator | Free Debt Elimination Planner"
- "Emergency Fund Calculator | Free Savings Planning Tool"
- "Savings Goal Calculator | Free Financial Goal Planner"
- "Loan Calculator | Free Mortgage & Auto Loan Tool"

### Description Templates:

- **Format**: "[Action verb] [benefit] with our professional [calculator type]. [Key feature]. [Secondary feature]. Free to use."
- **Length**: 150-160 characters
- **Keywords**: Include primary and secondary keywords
- **Call-to-Action**: Encourage use
