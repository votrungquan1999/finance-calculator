## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Ignoring Inflation
**Problem**: Withdrawing fixed dollar amounts
**Solution**: Increase withdrawals by inflation each year
**Impact**: Maintains purchasing power over time

### Mistake 2: Not Adjusting for Market Performance
**Problem**: Sticking to fixed percentage regardless of market
**Solution**: Use variable withdrawal strategies
**Impact**: Better portfolio preservation

### Mistake 3: Underestimating Longevity
**Problem**: Planning for 20-year retirement
**Solution**: Plan for 30+ year retirement
**Impact**: Avoid running out of money

### Mistake 4: Ignoring Tax Implications
**Problem**: Not considering tax impact of withdrawals
**Solution**: Plan tax-efficient withdrawal strategy
**Impact**: Maximize after-tax income

### Mistake 5: Not Having Backup Plans
**Problem**: Single withdrawal strategy
**Solution**: Multiple strategies for different scenarios
**Impact**: Better adaptability to changing circumstances
