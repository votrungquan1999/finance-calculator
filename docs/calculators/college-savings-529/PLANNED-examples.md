## State-Specific Examples

### California (No State Tax Deduction)

**529 Plan**: ScholarShare 529
- No state tax deduction
- Federal tax benefits only
- Low-cost investment options
- Automatic age-based allocation

**Tax Benefits**:
- Federal: Tax-free growth and withdrawals
- State: None
- Effective Return: 6% (no state tax benefit)

### New York (State Tax Deduction)

**529 Plan**: NY 529 Direct Plan
- State tax deduction up to $10,000/year
- Federal tax benefits
- Low-cost investment options
- Age-based allocation available

**Tax Benefits**:
- Federal: Tax-free growth and withdrawals
- State: 6.85% deduction on $10,000 = $685/year
- Effective Return: 6.3% (including state tax benefit)

### Texas (No State Tax)

**529 Plan**: Texas Tuition Promise Fund
- No state tax deduction (no state income tax)
- Federal tax benefits only
- Prepaid tuition option
- Age-based allocation available

**Tax Benefits**:
- Federal: Tax-free growth and withdrawals
- State: None (no state income tax)
- Effective Return: 6% (no state tax benefit)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Not Starting Early Enough
**Problem**: Waiting until child is 10+ to start saving
**Solution**: Start as early as possible, even with small amounts
**Impact**: Time is the biggest factor in college savings

### Mistake 2: Underestimating Costs
**Problem**: Using current costs instead of future costs
**Solution**: Use 5-6% education inflation rate
**Impact**: May fall short of actual need

### Mistake 3: Not Considering State Tax Benefits
**Problem**: Choosing 529 plan without considering state benefits
**Solution**: Research state tax benefits and contribution limits
**Impact**: Missing out on tax savings

### Mistake 4: Overcontributing to 529
**Problem**: Contributing more than needed for education
**Solution**: Consider other savings goals and 529 limits
**Impact**: Penalties on non-qualified withdrawals

### Mistake 5: Not Diversifying Education Savings
**Problem**: Relying only on 529 plans
**Solution**: Consider multiple education savings vehicles
**Impact**: More flexibility and options
