## Goal Setting Best Practices

### SMART Goals

**Specific**: "Save $5,000 for Hawaii vacation"
**Measurable**: Track progress monthly
**Achievable**: Based on realistic income
**Relevant**: Aligns with financial priorities
**Time-bound**: 12-month deadline

### Priority Setting

**Priority 1**: Essential goals (emergency fund, debt payoff)
**Priority 2**: Important goals (home, car, education)
**Priority 3**: Nice-to-have goals (vacation, entertainment)

### Timeline Planning

**Short-term**: 0-2 years (savings account)
**Medium-term**: 2-5 years (balanced approach)
**Long-term**: 5+ years (growth investments)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Unrealistic Timelines
**Problem**: Setting impossible deadlines
**Solution**: Calculate required monthly savings first
**Impact**: Avoids frustration and failure

### Mistake 2: Ignoring Inflation
**Problem**: Using current costs for future goals
**Solution**: Apply inflation rate to future purchases
**Impact**: Ensures adequate funding

### Mistake 3: Not Prioritizing Goals
**Problem**: Trying to save for everything at once
**Solution**: Rank goals by importance and timeline
**Impact**: Focuses resources on most important goals

### Mistake 4: Underestimating Costs
**Problem**: Not accounting for all expenses
**Solution**: Research actual costs and add buffer
**Impact**: Avoids shortfall at goal deadline

### Mistake 5: Not Adjusting for Life Changes
**Problem**: Sticking to rigid plans regardless of changes
**Solution**: Review and adjust goals regularly
**Impact**: Maintains relevance and achievability
