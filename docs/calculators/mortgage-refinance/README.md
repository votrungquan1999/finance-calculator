### ğŸ“Š Break-Even Factors

<details>
<summary><strong>ğŸ’° Closing Costs</strong></summary>

**Typical closing costs (2-5% of loan amount):**

- **Origination fees:** 0.5-1%
- **Title insurance:** $500-1,500
- **Appraisal:** $300-600
- **Recording fees:** $100-300
- **Attorney fees:** $500-1,000

**Ways to reduce:**

- Lender credits
- No-cost refinancing
- Shop around for services

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>ğŸ“ˆ Monthly Savings</strong></summary>

**Factors affecting savings:**

- **Rate difference:** Larger difference = more savings
- **Loan balance:** Higher balance = more savings
- **Term length:** Longer term = more savings
- **Payment frequency:** Bi-weekly vs monthly

**Calculate accurately:**

- Include all loan costs
- Factor in escrow changes
- Consider PMI changes

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>â° Time to Break-Even</strong></summary>

**Consider your timeline:**

- **Planning to move soon:** May not be worth it
- **Staying long-term:** More likely to benefit
- **Job stability:** Factor in relocation risk
- **Market conditions:** Consider rate trends

**Rule of thumb:** Break-even should be within 2-3 years

</details>
