## Error Messages & Validation

### Positive Framing

**Instead of**: "Invalid input"
**Use**: "Please enter a value between $1,000 and $10,000,000"

**Instead of**: "Error"
**Use**: "Please check your input and try again"

**Instead of**: "Cannot calculate"
**Use**: "Unable to calculate with current values"

### Helpful Explanations

**Interest Rate Validation**:
- "Interest rates typically range from 0.1% to 50% annually"
- "Please enter a realistic interest rate for your loan type"
- "Current mortgage rates are around 6-7%"

**Loan Amount Validation**:
- "Loan amounts typically range from $1,000 to $10,000,000"
- "Please enter a positive loan amount"
- "Consider your income and debt-to-income ratio"

**Term Validation**:
- "Loan terms typically range from 1 month to 50 years"
- "Please enter a realistic loan term"
- "Consider your budget and financial goals"

### Actionable Instructions

**Missing Required Fields**:
- "Please fill in exactly 4 of the 5 fields to calculate the missing value"
- "Leave one field empty to solve for that value"
- "Choose which value you want to calculate"

**Calculation Errors**:
- "Unable to calculate with these values. Please check your inputs."
- "The calculation resulted in an invalid value. Please try different inputs."
- "Please ensure all values are realistic and positive."

### Error Message Examples

**Input Validation**:
- "Please enter a loan amount between $1,000 and $10,000,000"
- "Interest rate must be between 0.1% and 50%"
- "Loan term must be between 1 month and 50 years"
- "Please enter a positive number"

**Calculation Errors**:
- "Unable to calculate payment with current values"
- "Please check that your inputs are realistic"
- "The calculation resulted in an invalid amount"

**System Errors**:
- "Something went wrong. Please try again."
- "Unable to process your request. Please refresh the page."
- "Calculation failed. Please check your inputs and try again."
